Is Synara The Right Partner For You?

We're dedicated to building powerful partnerships. To ensure success, it's important to know if we're the right solution. Here are some scenarios where we might not be the best fit.

Physical-Only Tasks

Our AI operates in the digital world. We don't automate physical robotics or assembly lines.

No API Access

If your systems are proprietary legacy software with no way for us to connect, our hands are tied.

Inability to Collaborate

Our process requires partnership. We need your team's input during the initial setup phase to succeed.

Tech-Resistant Culture

If your team is completely resistant to adopting new technology, implementing an AI agent will be a struggle.

Unreliable Data

Our AI relies on data. If the information needed is consistently inaccurate or inaccessible, automation is impossible.

One-Time Projects

Our value shines in automating ongoing, repeatable processes, not single, non-recurring tasks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've got answers. If you don't see your question here, feel free to contact us.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Security is our top priority. We are SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, and ISO 27001 compliant. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we use secure, role-based access controls for any integrations with your systems.

How is this different from a tool like Zapier?

Tools like Zapier are fantastic for simple, trigger-based automations (if A, then B). We handle complex, multi-step workflows that require reasoning and decision-making, like interpreting the content of an email or PDF. We are a "done-for-you" service where we build and manage the automations, whereas Zapier is a "do-it-yourself" platform.

What if I have a custom tool that's not listed?

No problem. Our Enterprise plan includes custom integrations. If your tool has an API, we can almost certainly build a connection for your AI agent to interact with it. Book a call to discuss your specific needs.

What does the 14-day deployment process involve?

**Days 1-3:** Kickoff call & workflow mapping. **Days 4-10:** Our team configures and tests your AI agent in a secure sandbox. **Days 11-12:** You review the agent's performance and provide feedback. **Day 14:** Your agent goes live!

Is there a limit to how complex a process you can automate?

Our AI agents can handle incredibly complex, multi-step workflows with conditional logic. The best way to know for sure is to book a free consultation. We love a good challenge and will be transparent about what is and isn't possible.
